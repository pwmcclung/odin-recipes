Prime Rib

Description:

This is a specific formula for achieving a perfectly pink prime rib cooked to medium rare. This recipe provides 1752 calories per serving, 72.5 grams of protein, 1 gram of carbohydrate, and 160.6 grams of fat.

Ingredients

  • 4 pound prime rib roast
  • 1/4/ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • kosher salt, applied liberally
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence

Steps

  1. Place rib roast on plate and bring to room temperature, about 4 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
  3. Combine butter, pepper, and herbes de Provence in a bowl; mix until well blended. Spread butter mixture evenly over entire roast. Season roast generously with kosher salt.
  4. Roast the 4-pound prime rib in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Turn the oven off, and leaving the roast in the oven with the door closed, let the roast sit in the oven for 2 hours. Remove the Roast from the oven, slice, serve.
