Meat Loaf

Description:

Enjoy meatloaf, just like Mom used to make. One serving of this classic dish contains 410 Calories, 28.4 grams of protein, 22.3 grams of carbohydrates, and 22.7 grams of fat.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper/li>
  • 3 slices of bread, crumbled
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef/li>
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1/2/ cup shredded carrot
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup/li>
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and ground black pepper in a large bowl. Add crumbled bread and stir until disolved. Mix ground beef, onion, cheddar cheese, and carrot into bread mixture; transfer mixture to a 9x5-inch loaf pan. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard in small bowl; spread over meat mixture.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 1 to 1 1/4 hours. An instant read meat thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
